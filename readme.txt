A simple and powerfull plugin to automatically send your posts to your Facebook Profile or Page.


== Installation ==

Extract the zip file and just drop the contents in the wp-content/plugins/ directory of your WordPress installation and then activate the Plugin from Plugins page.

Go to Settings > Post to Facebook and authorize your account, then select the accounts in which to publish and click save. 

== Description ==

Easy Post to Facebook help you to submit your posts to Facebook automatically, simply authorize your account and select the pages (or profiles) that you want to publish your posts.
